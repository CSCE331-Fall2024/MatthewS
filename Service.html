Construction

Over the past three summers, I have worked in construction, gaining hands-on experience in various tasks such as repairing fascia, building deck roofs, and operating a skid steer to level ground. My work in the small town of Centerpoint, TX, has enhanced the aesthetic appeal of several homes, contributing to increased tourism at the nearby river and boosting overall traffic through the city.

Working On Vehicles

Having a working level of knowledge in repairing vehicles, many people have asked me to fix their cars, which I often do for a significantly cheaper price than that of a mechanic shop, if not for free. For example, I recently changed a friend's alternator in their car for free, which was located in a severely annoying spot (picture above). In this instance and several others, I have allowed people to return to their daily lives without the fear of financial burden.

Painting

Similar to my experience with cars, I have also provided painting services at a significantly lower cost. I have painted three homes, including one nearly entirely on my own, which took me 2-3 weeks. My process involves thorough scraping, caulking, and painting, which has not only helped homeowners reduce financial stress but, in one case, contributed to an increased selling price of their home.

Campus Org's

Although I have not yet been involved in many on-campus organizations, I am eager to join some to enhance my portfolio and gain valuable experience.
